Cereal

  • Time: 3-5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients/Tools Quantity
Milk1/2 cup or more based on preferences
Store bought cerealexact same amount as milk plus 1/4 cup
Bowl1
Spoon1

Step 1: Pour milk into a microwave safe bowl.

Step 2: Microwave milk for 1 minute.

Step 3: Add cereal.

Step 4: Wait for 2-4 minutes.

Step 5: Enjoy after adding spoon!

Waffles

  • Time: 5-7 minutes
  • Serves: As many as you want!
Ingredients/Tools Quantity
Waffle MixDepends on how much you would like to make
WaterAbout 1-1 and 1/4 cup is good
Bowl1
Mixer1
Maple syrup2 tablespoons * the amount you want to make
Fork1
Waffle Maker1
ButterDepends on how much you are making; 1/8*amount of times batter is used

Step 1: Put waffle mix and water in the bowl.

Step 2: Use the mixer to mix all of your ingredients together, making sure not to mix it too much. It should have the consistency of batter.

Step 3: Turn on waffle maker and wait for however long it takes your waffle maker to get hot enough to melt butter.

Step 4: Add butter to your waffle mixer, about an 1/8 of the stick should be good enough.

Step 5: Pour your waffle batter evenly on the mixer, making sure no corner or anything is cut. Make sure you don't over pour it. Wait for 5-10 ish minutes.

Step 6: Repeat step 4 and 5 until you run out of batter.

Step 7: Pour maple syrup, about 2 tablespoons per waffle.

Step 8: Enjoy your waffle, and eat it with a fork!
